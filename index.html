True Facts About the Programmer

I have had a career in the support side of IT for 15+ years, doing desktop support for Windows, Linux, UNIX at various points, as well as hardware testing and inbound telephone support. I recently decided to shift over to the development side of the IT world to professionally develop my programming habit. I chose to attend Codefellows at their Portland campus to get my start in Javascript full stack web development.

Skills

Since March of 2016 I have been focusing on JavaScript development, learning ES5. I've accumulated a fair number of skills in a short time, including a fair understanding of closures, hoisting, functional and object-oriented programming, and DOM manipulation, I've also become fairly adept at HTML and CSS design.

Fun fact: to date, I have written lines of production code!
